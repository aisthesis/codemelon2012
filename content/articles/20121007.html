Playground: Clouds

10-07-2012

This playground will explore how to create 3D clouds using CSS3 and JavaScript. The experiment is based on Jaume Sánchez's CSS3D Clouds.

Here are some ways you can interact with the clouds:

  • Press the spacebar to generate a new cloud group.
  • Hold the SHIFT key and move your mouse to rotate around the clouds.
  • In Chrome and Safari, you can also zoom in or out by holding SHIFT and scrolling with the mouse wheel.

I also changed the speed variable of the original tutorial so that the clouds don't always rotate in the same direction. The JavaScript used to generate the clouds can be viewed on the codemelon2012 GitHub.
